Hair Texture

First, it’s important to understand your hair texture. Different hair textures require different hair care routines!

This is important because the more curly your hair texture, the less your scalp’s natural oils will slide down the shaft of your hair strands and moisturize them. This means, the curlier your hair is, the more your hair will need to be moisturized. Learn about each of the hair textures: 3A , 3B, 3C, 4A, 4B and 4C
