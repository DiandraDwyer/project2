Hair Texture

Next, it’s important to understand your hair texture. Different hair textures require different hair care routines!

This is important because the more curly your hair texture, the less your scalp’s natural oils will slide down the shaft of your hair strands and moisturize them. This means, the curlier your hair is, the more your hair will need to be moisturized.

Moisturizers

Moisturizers are vital to the health of your hair! Without them, it can become dry, brittle and break off at the ends. Knowing what type of moisturizer to use however, is just as important for both the health of your hair and body.

Chemicals and Dyes

Chemicals and Dyes often come at the end of the ingredients list, which means proportionately there are less of them present in the product than other ingredients. However, this does not mean they do not have a huge impact on your health!

These are

  • Propanol Alcohol
  • SD Alcohol
  • SD Alcohol 40
  • Sulfur
  • Sulfates
  • Propyl Alcohol
  • Ethanol Alcohol
  • Isopropyl Alcohol
  • Color dyes
